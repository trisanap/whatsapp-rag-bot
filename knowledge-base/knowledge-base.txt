## Tips for Building Your Knowledge Base

1. **Be specific** — vague answers lead to vague bot responses.
2. **Use Q&A format** — it maps directly to how users ask questions.
3. **Include edge cases** — what happens when something goes wrong?
4. **Keep it updated** — stale knowledge leads to wrong answers.
5. **Test with real user questions** — after indexing, try actual queries
   and refine the content based on where the bot struggles.
